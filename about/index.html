About Me

Donato Maragno

I enjoy working at the intersection of AI and mathematical optimization to turn complex data into scalable decision-making systems that drive real business value.

I worked at MIT and Harvard on projects ranging from democratizing optimization to personalized radiotherapy planning. Currently, I work as a Scientist at Amazon on projects spanning supply chain management, logistics, production planning, and sustainability. I build optimization frameworks that are robust under uncertainty, practical and scalable for small, medium, and large industries, and able of delivering measurable impact.
